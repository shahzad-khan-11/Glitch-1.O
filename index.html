About EcoBin

EcoBin was developed for the GLITCH 1.0 Hackathon under the Environment & Sustainability category.

Transforming E-Waste Management in Patna

Every year, millions of electronic devices are discarded unsafely, contributing to environmental pollution and health hazards. EcoBin addresses this critical issue by connecting citizens with verified e-waste collection centers through an intuitive, map-based platform.

Our solution leverages Google Maps API to provide real-time location data, making it easier than ever for residents of Patna to find nearby collection points and dispose of their electronic waste responsibly.
